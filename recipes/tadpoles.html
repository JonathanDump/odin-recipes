Tadpoles in vinegar

Description

These are really fun to make, either as worms or poured into differently shaped silicone moulds, if you have them (we got very into little bears, for instance).

Ingredients

  • 600 g. fresh tadpoles
  • 20 ml. vinegar
  • 20 ml. oil
  • 3 pinches of black pepper

Directions

  1. Wash the tadpoles
  2. Let them dry
  3. Pour vinegar and oil
  4. Add pepper
  5. Mix
