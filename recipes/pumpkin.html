Pumpkin with worms

Description

These are really fun to make, either as worms or poured into differently shaped silicone moulds, if you have them (we got very into little bears, for instance).

Ingredients

  • 400 g. row worms
  • 2 pieces of garlic
  • Rotten pumpkin

Directions

  1. Cut off a hat of pumpkin
  2. Pick out pumpkin inners
  3. Fill pumpkin with worms
  4. Add garlic
